# How to delete your callmeZ account and data

**Last updated:** [INSERT LAUNCH DATE]

> **Founder note (delete before publishing):** Google Play requires this to be reachable at a **public URL** that you enter in the Play Console. It can live at something like `callmez.app/delete-account`. Keep it short and plain.

You can delete your callmeZ account at any time. Here's how, and what happens to your data.

## Delete from inside the app (fastest)

1. Open callmeZ and go to **Settings → Privacy & Data**.
2. Tap **Delete Account**.
3. Confirm. Your account is deactivated immediately.

## Delete by email

If you can't access the app, email **help@callmez.app** from the phone number or with details that let us verify your account, and ask us to delete it. We'll confirm and process the request.

## What happens to your data

- **Deactivated immediately.** As soon as you delete, your account and its data become inaccessible in the app.
- **Kept for 30 days, then permanently erased.** For 30 days you can email **help@callmez.app** to recover your account. After 30 days, your data — including your profile, conversations, and everything the AI remembered — is **permanently purged and cannot be recovered.**
- **Some minimal records** may be retained only where the law requires (for example, limited fraud-prevention or transaction records), for only as long as required.

## Want to keep your account but remove specific things?

You don't have to delete everything:

- **Make the AI forget a specific fact:** open the **"what Z remembers"** screen and delete individual items.
- **Export your data:** go to **Settings → Privacy & Data → Export**.

Questions? **help@callmez.app**
