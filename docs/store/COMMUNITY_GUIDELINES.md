# callmeZ — Community Guidelines

**Last updated:** [INSERT LAUNCH DATE]

callmeZ is a place to think, talk, learn, and play — with AI characters and with real people. Public rooms and DMs only work if everyone treats each other decently. These guidelines apply everywhere people can see or reach each other: public rooms, group chats, and direct messages.

## Do

- Be curious, be real, and give people room to be themselves.
- Disagree well. Debate the idea, not the person.
- Report anything that breaks these rules (see below).

## Don't

- **Harass or bully.** No threats, targeted abuse, stalking, or pile-ons.
- **Spread hate.** No attacks on people based on religion, caste, ethnicity, nationality, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics.
- **Post illegal or harmful content.** Nothing that breaks the law, promotes violence, or facilitates harm.
- **Never involve minors in sexual content.** This is an absolute line. We report where the law requires.
- **Post sexual or explicit content** in shared spaces.
- **Spam or scam.** No unsolicited ads, phishing, or bulk messaging.
- **Impersonate** other people, brands, or callmeZ staff.
- **Share others' private information** without their consent.

## Games are for fun, not money

Games use play-money credits with no real-world value. Don't try to trade, sell, or cash out virtual credits, and don't use callmeZ to arrange real-money gambling.

## Moderation

- **Room creators are moderators.** If you create a public room, you can remove (kick) people who break the rules.
- **We act on reports.** We may remove content, limit features, or suspend or close accounts that break these guidelines — sometimes immediately for serious violations.

## How to report

- Use the **report** option on a message, room, or user, **or**
- Email **help@callmez.app** with details (who, where, and what happened).

We review reports and take action as needed. Serious safety issues — especially anything involving minors — get priority.

Thanks for helping keep callmeZ a place worth being.
